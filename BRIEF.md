# GiveHugz v4 — Project Brief

## Business Info
- **Name**: GiveHugz
- **Industry**: Weighted Stuffed Animals / Emotional Wellness (DTC)
- **URL**: https://givehugz.co
- **Type**: Shopify headless (Next.js frontend)
- **Contact**: support@givehugz.co
- **Social**: Instagram @giveahugz, TikTok @giveahugz

## Project Type
Headless Shopify rebuild — v4 of the site, testing visual-research-driven methodology.

## Products
- 43 weighted stuffed animals (exclude "message" handle at $0)
- Regular Hugz: $59.99, Mini Hugz: $39.99, Gift Cards: $10+
- Each animal has a name and personality
- Excellent product photography (4-21 images per product)

## Shopify Config
- Products endpoint: `givehugz.co/products.json`
- Collections endpoint: `givehugz.co/collections.json`
- Checkout: Redirect to `givehugz.co/cart/{variantId}:{quantity}`
- Collections: All Hugz, Best Sellers, Mini Hugz, Bundles, Featured, Exclusive, etc.

## Brand DNA
- Colors: teal #108474, deep teal #024a4a, cream #fff8e1, soft pink #ffcfc7, mint #edf5f5
- Fonts: Petrona (serif headlines) + Poppins (sans body) — PRESERVE
- Heart-shaped favicon, heart motif throughout
- Warm, nurturing, NOT childish
- Mental health mission: Inspiring Children Foundation partnership
- Press: Today Show, USA Today, Rolling Stone, Cosmopolitan, Yahoo

## Site Architecture
- Homepage, PDP, Collection pages, Mission, FAQ, Contact, Wholesale, Blog, Cart
