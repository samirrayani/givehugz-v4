# Soul Brief — GiveHugz

There is a specific kind of comfort that only comes from pressure — the weight of a hand on your shoulder, the full-body release of being held. GiveHugz makes that feeling portable. These aren't stuffed animals. They're the physical shape of being held by someone who isn't going anywhere.

The brand lives at the intersection of two truths most people feel but rarely say out loud: that anxiety is a physical experience (the chest tightens, the breath shortens, the body curls inward), and that relief is also physical (weight grounds you, pressure slows the heartbeat, warmth tells your nervous system that you're safe). Each Hugz is a 2-pound answer to the question your body has been asking.

What's poetic is the paradox: something designed to feel like another person's embrace, delivered through the simplest possible form — a soft animal with a name and a weight that settles into you like a held breath finally released.
