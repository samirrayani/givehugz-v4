# Story Arc — GiveHugz Homepage

## Beat 1: Recognition
"You know that feeling." Arrive and immediately feel seen — the tension you carry, the nights that won't settle, the way anxiety lives in your body. Not clinical. Visceral. The visitor exhales because someone finally described it right.

## Beat 2: Revelation
"There's a reason weight helps." The shift from emotional recognition to physical understanding. Deep pressure therapy isn't new — it's why weighted blankets work, why babies are swaddled, why a tight hug calms a panic attack. The science is simple and ancient.

## Beat 3: Meeting
"They have names. They have weight. They're waiting." The first encounter with the products — but framed as introductions, not transactions. You don't "shop" a Hugz. You meet one. The warmth of character, the specificity of each personality.

## Beat 4: Trust
"50,000 people sleep better tonight." The quiet confidence of social proof — not shouting, but steady. Real voices, real relief. Press logos not as bragging but as reassurance. The moment doubt dissolves.

## Beat 5: Purpose
"Every Hugz holds something bigger." The mission reveal — mental health advocacy, the Inspiring Children Foundation, the belief that comfort isn't a luxury. This isn't CSR window dressing. This is why the company exists.

## Beat 6: Invitation
"Take one home." The gentlest possible conversion. Not "BUY NOW" — an invitation to bring this feeling into your life or gift it to someone who needs it. The emotional temperature is warm, open, unhurried.
